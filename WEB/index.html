About UniPlus Street Foundation

At Uniplus Street Foundation, we are dedicated to empowering individuals and transforming communities. Through our initiatives, we provide essential resources, education, and opportunities to those in need. Our mission is to foster sustainable growth, promote social equity, and inspire positive change. By focusing on building skills, creating connections, and uplifting communities, we work toward a future where everyone has the chance to thrive.

  • Give Back to Society
  • Build Skills for the future.
  • Give and Experience

As an emerging foundation, we are passionate about making a meaningful impact and building a strong foundation for lasting change. With a focus on innovative programs and collaborative efforts, we are committed to empowering communities, fostering growth, and creating opportunities that inspire hope and transformation.

Our Goals

With a team of mind-like individuals at our disposal we are determined to achieve thses goals

Empower Communities

We aim to uplift communities by providing access to education, vocational training, and mentorship programs. Our focus is on fostering self-reliance and creating opportunities for sustainable growth. Through empowerment, we strive to break the cycle of poverty and inspire lasting change.

Inspire Positive Change

We aim to foster inclusivity, equity, and hope by addressing community needs. Through our programs, we uplift individuals and drive meaningful transformation. Together, we work towards a brighter and more harmonious future.

Promote Collaboration

We prioritize building strong alliances with organizations, stakeholders, and communities to amplify efforts, share resources, and create sustainable solutions. By working together, we maximize our impact and drive lasting positive change.

Create Opportunity for All

We aim to remove barriers to education, employment, and resources for marginalized groups. Through training, mentorship, and support, we create pathways to economic independence. Our goal is to ensure everyone has the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Build a Brighter Future

We empower individuals with skills and opportunities, paving the way for a more inclusive and prosperous tomorrow. By focusing on education, mentorship, and community-driven growth, we aim to create lasting change and ensure that future generations are equipped to succeed.

Set Milestones

We define clear milestones to track progress and ensure we stay on schedule. Regular reviews help us assess achievements, identify areas for improvement, and stay aligned with our long-term goals. This approach ensures continuous growth and impactful results.

DZORWULU SPECIAL SCHOOL

The Dzorwulu Special School provides special care, treatment and training to kids living with autism. The institution has over the years also provided relief to parents of special kids who dread the stigmatisation of autistic children in Ghana. Many kids have discovered their hidden talents here, at the Dzorwulu Special School.
